## Decisions Made
- Fire-and-forget notification calls use `CancellationToken.None` because the notification should complete even when the parent health check operation is cancelled — passing the parent token would silently discard the notification on cancellation
- LocalTimestamp renders an em-dash (`&mdash;`) rather than UTC time during prerender to prevent the "UTC flash" UX artifact where times appear in UTC briefly before converting to local time
- Playwright `Expect(Page.Locator(".mud-main-content")).ToBeVisibleAsync()` replaces `Task.Delay(1000)` — this is deterministic (waits for actual content) not time-bound (risks flakiness)
